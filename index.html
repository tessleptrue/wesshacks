Welcome. WesShacks will be a platform for students to rate and review the woodframe houses at Wesleyan University. Picking a house to live in can be difficult and having a reviews would provide to needed informating to make a decision. While personal preferences vary, knowing what previous residents thought about their home would be a tremendous help.

WesShacks will provide to following services:

  1. Reviews and comments on each house
  2. Current residents may post their review
  3. There will be a questions section so that the current residents may answer any questions about their living space
