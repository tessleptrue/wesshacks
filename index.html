Welcome. WesShacks is a platform for users to browse, review, and rate the woodframe houses at Wesleyan University. Picking a house to live in can be difficult and having a reviews would provide to needed informating to make a decision. While personal preferences vary, knowing what previous residents thought about their home would be a tremendous help.

BROWSE

  • Our goal is to make a directory of the woodframe houses at Wesleyan University.
  • We aim to provide detailed information about each house.
  • Users can explore various houses and their features.

REVIEW

  • After users create an account, they can write reviews about a house they currently live, a house they used to live in, or a house they have visited for an event.
  • Reviews from users who live/lived in the house being reviewed will be tagged as resident reviews.
  • Reviews from users who have visited the house being reviewed will be tagged as guest reviews.
  • Reviews help future residents make informed decisions about where they want to live.

RATE

  • Users can rate different aspects of the houses on a scale from 1 to 5.
  • Ratings contribute to the overall house ratings.
  • Higher ratings can attract more interest from potential residents.
